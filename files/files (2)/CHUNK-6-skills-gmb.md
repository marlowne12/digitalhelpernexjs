# GMB Audit Skill

## Purpose
Systematic audit of Google My Business profiles with actionable recommendations.

## Audit Checklist

### 1. Basic Information (Critical)
- [ ] Business name matches real-world name exactly
- [ ] Primary category is most specific option available
- [ ] Secondary categories cover all services (max 9)
- [ ] Address is accurate and consistent with website
- [ ] Service area defined (if service-area business)
- [ ] Phone number matches website
- [ ] Website URL is correct
- [ ] Hours are accurate including special hours
- [ ] Business description uses full 750 characters
- [ ] Opening date is set

### 2. Visual Content
- [ ] Logo uploaded (square, high quality)
- [ ] Cover photo set (landscape, represents business)
- [ ] Minimum 10 photos total
- [ ] Photos categorized:
  - [ ] Interior (if applicable)
  - [ ] Exterior
  - [ ] Team/staff
  - [ ] Products/services in action
- [ ] Photos updated in last 30 days
- [ ] No stock photos
- [ ] Video content (bonus)

### 3. Products & Services
- [ ] Products section populated (if applicable)
- [ ] Services section with descriptions
- [ ] Pricing included where possible
- [ ] Links to relevant website pages

### 4. Attributes
- [ ] All relevant attributes selected
- [ ] Health & safety attributes current
- [ ] Accessibility attributes set
- [ ] Amenities marked
- [ ] Service options noted

### 5. Posts & Updates
- [ ] Posted within last 7 days
- [ ] Mix of post types (offers, updates, events)
- [ ] CTAs included in posts
- [ ] Images in posts
- [ ] Consistent posting schedule

### 6. Reviews
- [ ] Overall rating (note number)
- [ ] Total review count
- [ ] Review count vs. competitors
- [ ] Response rate to reviews
- [ ] Response time to reviews
- [ ] Quality of responses (personalized vs. template)
- [ ] Recent review velocity (last 30 days)

### 7. Q&A Section
- [ ] Common questions pre-populated by owner
- [ ] All questions answered
- [ ] Answers are helpful and complete

- [ ] Messaging enabled (if team can handle)

- [ ] Response time acceptable

# GMB Review Response Skill

## Purpose
Generate appropriate, personalized review responses that build trust and support local SEO.

## Response Principles

1. **Respond to ALL reviews** - positive and negative
2. **Personalize every response** - reference specific details mentioned
3. **Include keywords naturally** - service type, location when appropriate
4. **Keep it concise** - 2-4 sentences for positive, more for negative
5. **End with forward motion** - invite back, offer next step

## Response Templates by Rating

### 5-Star Reviews

**Template A - Service Specific:**
```
[Name], thank you for the wonderful feedback about your [service mentioned]! We're thrilled that [specific positive point]. Our team in [City] takes pride in [relevant value]. We look forward to serving you again!
```

**Template B - General Positive:**
```
Thank you so much for taking the time to share your experience, [Name]! It means a lot to our [City] team to hear that [reference something specific]. We're here whenever you need [service type] again!
```

**Template C - Enthusiastic Customer:**
```
[Name], reviews like yours make our day! We loved helping you with [service/project] and are so glad [specific outcome]. Thanks for trusting [Business Name]. See you next time!
```

### 4-Star Reviews

```
Thank you for the great review, [Name]! We're glad [positive aspect mentioned] met your expectations. We're always looking to improve - if there's anything that would have made it 5 stars, we'd love to hear. Thanks for choosing [Business Name] in [City]!
```

### 3-Star Reviews

```
[Name], thank you for your honest feedback. We're glad [any positive mentioned], but sorry we didn't fully meet your expectations regarding [issue if mentioned]. We'd love the chance to make it right - please reach out to [contact method] so we can discuss. Your satisfaction matters to us.
```

### 2-Star Reviews

```
[Name], we're sorry to hear about your experience. This isn't the standard we hold ourselves to at [Business Name]. We'd genuinely like to understand what went wrong and make it right. Please contact [Owner/Manager Name] directly at [phone/email] - we want to resolve this for you.
```

### 1-Star Reviews

```
[Name], we sincerely apologize for your experience. This is concerning to us and not reflective of the service we strive to provide. [Owner/Manager Name] would like to speak with you personally to understand what happened and find a resolution. Please reach out at [contact] at your earliest convenience. We want to make this right.
```

## Personalization Requirements

**Always include:**
- Reviewer's name (first name only)
- Reference to specific service/product/interaction if mentioned
- Business name (1x)
- City/location (1x, naturally placed)

**For positive reviews, also mention:**
- Specific employee praised (if applicable)
- Specific outcome or result mentioned

**For negative reviews, also include:**
- Acknowledgment of specific issue
- Owner/manager name for escalation
- Direct contact method

## Keywords to Include Naturally

- Business name
- City/service area
- Primary service type (when mentioned in review)

**Never stuff keywords** - they should flow naturally

## Things to NEVER Include

- Defensive language
- Blame on the customer
- Excuses
- Competitor mentions
- Pricing discussions in public
- Private customer information
- Discount offers (handle privately)

## Escalation Rules

**Immediately alert Slack #review-alerts if:**
- 1-star review
- 2-star review mentioning employee by name
- Any review alleging safety or legal issues
- Review that appears fake or from competitor
- Review with potential to go viral (detailed, well-written complaint)

## Response Timing

| Rating | Target Response Time |
|--------|---------------------|
| 1-2 stars | Within 4 hours (business hours) |
| 3 stars | Within 12 hours |
| 4-5 stars | Within 24 hours |
